Smart Lighting

Smart lighting allows you to control the lights in your home using your phone, remote control, or voice commands. It helps save energy and improves comfort.

Example Application

You can turn on the lights automatically when you enter the room, or control all lights using your mobile phone. Smart lighting can also adjust brightness depending on time of day.
